---
title: "Supported Languages"
---
The Speech-to-Text solution supports transcription in multiple languages, tailored for diverse regional and linguistic needs:

- `en` - Indian English
- `hi` - Hindi
- `hi_en` - Hinglish
- `bn` - Bengali
- `gu` - Gujarati
- `kn` - Kannada
- `ml` - Malayalam
- `mr` - Marathi
- `pa` - Punjabi
- `ta` - Tamil
- `te` - Telugu
- `as` - Assamese
- `or` - Odia
